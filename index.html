About Us

Who Am I?

I am an early childhood educator who specializes in teaching and developing bilingual programs (English - Indonesian) based on the Islamic way of education. I have more than four years of experience and I have been responsible for various areas such as developing educational programs for young children including Islamic based curriculum, designing media (digital and non-digital), teaching methods, lesson planning, classroom management, child development reports, book advisor and editor, I also do some related jobs such as story telling, MC and speaker for any children events.

What I do?

Here are some of my expertise

Teaching

I am able to teach early childhood both privately and in peer groups, online and on-site, throughout my teaching career, I have taught children locally and internationally, from people with limited finances to celebrities, I teach in several areas, such as, English and Arabic (literacy), math, arts and crafts, physical education, general sciences, general Islamic sciences, pre-Qur'an literacy.




Eventual / Thematic Teacher

I also have other teaching responsibilities outside of the institution where I work, such as being an on-call teacher for certain subjects or being a speaker on certain topics.


Designing Teaching Tools

I am able to design and develop teaching tools such as lesson plans for both online and onsite classes, teaching aids / learning media (digital and non-digital) tailored to children's learning needs. Throughout my teaching, I use fun learning teaching methods that incorporate various approaches such as Islamic Montessori, fitrah based education.

Classroom Environment Management

I was able to evaluate and design a classroom taking into account aesthetic and ergonomic values to make it comfortable and safe for students to learn, starting from the decoration, analysis of the ratio of the room to the number of students, the brightness level of the screen and audio volume, the type of chair and whiteboard, etc.

Storytelling

I have been doing storytelling since I was in high school, in some subjects I usually convey through storytelling, besides I also work in this field to fill children's activities outside the institution I work in.

Book Advisor & Editor

In this case, I make suggestions regarding the incoming material according to age and developmental level, as well as evaluating and editing the types of games or activities in the book, searching for hadith or verses related to the material, and translating the language.
